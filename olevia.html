About Olevia

Small-batch blends,
made with care in Alberta.

Olevia is a handcrafted aromatherapy brand rooted in botanical tradition. Every blend is made in small batches — never mass-produced — using quality essential oils chosen for their aroma and character. You'll find us at local Alberta markets and right here online.

Have a question about a blend, want to know what's in stock, or just want to talk oils? Reach out — we love hearing from you.
